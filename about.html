Hello there!

I'm Katie Pustolski, a game developer in Los Angeles, CA

My name is Katie and I am currently a software engineer in the games industry. My personal projects tend to lean toward a cute, quirky, and at times absurdly humorous style. If I can make a person smile, laugh, and learn with my projects, then I consider that a job well done.

In 2015, I graduated from the Rochester Institute of Technology in Rochester, New York with a BSc in Game Design & Development. I then moved directly to Los Angeles, California (talk about a drastic change in climate) where I got a MFA in Interactive Media at the University of Southern California in 2018. I currently reside in Los Angeles, CA.
