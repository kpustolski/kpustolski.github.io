Professional Projects

Released projects that I have contributed to at various studios.

Marvel Snap

Marvel Snap is an award winning, digital collectable card game developed by Second Dinner in which players build a deck of twelve Marvel characters and battle against one another to increase (or decrease) their seasonal rank.

More information on the game can be found at https://marvelsnap.com/.

Fictif

Fictif is a collection of otomie inspired interactive visual novels in which players play as the main character and choose which character they like to romance throughout each story.

Fictif was initially developed by a company called Nix Hydra, but is now owned and maintained by Dorian.live.

Potions Please!

Potions Please! was a narrative based adventure game where the player acted as an apprentice to a traveling potion maker. The gameplay focused around collecting ingredients through mini games and progressing the narrative by brewing potions for the townfolk in various locations.

Potions Please! had a limited release and unfortunetly was shelved prior to Nix Hydra's acquisition by Dorian.live.

The Arcana

Taking inspiration from tarot and otome style love stories, The Arcana is a mobile interactive visual novel where players play as the main character and choose one of six characters to romance throughout the main story.

The Arcana was initially developed by a company called Nix Hydra, but is now owned and maintained by Dorian.live.
