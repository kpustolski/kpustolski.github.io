Lindolpho's Box of Ephemera

Visit vignettes of writer Lindolpho Espeschit's life by piecing together a collage of images, letters, and sounds in this short interactive experience.

Lindolpho's Box of Ephemera was created for IndieCade's Hidden Hero's Game Jam back in February 2023. I teamed up with my former University of Southern California classmate Gabriela Purri Gomes and her sister Giulia Purri Gomes to create an interactive experience based on their great-grandfather, Lindolpho Espeschit.

This project is available to play for free on itch.io.

More information

Born in 1896, Lindolpho was a journalist and writer from Brazil who published his first book and biography at the age of 98 years old, four years before his death at age 102. He was chosen for the rich stories and accounts he left behind in his book, and his great-granddaughters' wish to explore his legacy and the history of their German ancestors' migration to Brazil. While our main source of inspiration was his book, we also interviewed one of his last remaining daughters and explored a large archive of photos, footage and memorabilia different members of the family have kept along the years.

We all collaborated in designing this experience. As the point engineer on the team, I handled most of building the project in the Unity Game Engine while my teammates gathered information on Lindolpho, created the assets, and handled the Portuguese translation used in the project. The most challenging part of this project was that we were spreadout across different countries and time zones.
